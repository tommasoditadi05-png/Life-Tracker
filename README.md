
# LifeTracker

## AI-Powered Personal Growth and Self-Improvement Platform

LifeTracker is a personal project designed to help people improve different areas of their lives through artificial intelligence, progress tracking, and personalized action plans.

The idea originated from a challenge that many students and young people face: procrastination, lack of discipline, uncertainty about the future, and difficulty turning intentions into concrete actions. LifeTracker aims to provide users with a structured path for personal development by transforming long-term goals into achievable daily actions.

## Vision

LifeTracker is more than a productivity tool. It is designed as an intelligent personal coach capable of understanding the user's goals, challenges, insecurities, and aspirations. Through continuous interaction, the system generates personalized improvement plans and adapts them according to the user's progress and feedback.

The project is based on a simple principle:

> Consistent action, even in small steps, can create significant long-term change.

## Main Features

* AI-generated personal development plans
* Daily progress tracking
* Personalized feedback system
* Performance scoring across multiple life areas
* Progress visualization through charts and statistics
* Positive and negative streak monitoring
* Historical archive for long-term comparison
* Integrated agenda and goal management

## Areas of Improvement

LifeTracker can support users in different aspects of life, including:

* Education and study habits
* Productivity and discipline
* Social relationships
* Physical well-being
* Personal organization
* Skill development
